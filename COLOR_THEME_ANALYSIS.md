1. **#e56481** - Pink/Rose (Coral Pink)
2. **#499bcb** - Sky Blue (Bright Blue)

---

## Theme Assessment

### ✅ **STRENGTHS**

1. **Vibrant & Energetic**: Your palette is lively and attention-grabbing, perfect for a modern home services platform
2. **Good Color Range**: You have warm (pink, orange) and cool (blue, navy) tones, plus a fresh accent (green)
3. **Brand Personality**: The colors convey:
   - **Pink (#e56481)**: Friendly, approachable, caring
   - **Blue (#499bcb)**: Trustworthy, professional, reliable
   - **Orange (#f69e34)**: Energetic, enthusiastic, action-oriented
   - **Navy (#2d3869)**: Professional, stable, trustworthy
   - **Green (#9fc95b)**: Fresh, growth, positive energy

### ⚠️ **CONCERNS & RECOMMENDATIONS**

#### 1. **Color Harmony**
- **Current State**: The palette mixes warm and cool tones, which can work but needs careful balance
- **Recommendation**: Use a **60-30-10 rule**:
  - **60%**: Navy (#2d3869) - Primary/dominant color (headers, main backgrounds)
  - **30%**: Blue (#499bcb) - Secondary color (buttons, accents)
  - **10%**: Orange (#f69e34), Pink (#e56481), Green (#9fc95b) - Accent colors (CTAs, highlights)

#### 2. **Accessibility & Contrast**
- **Issue**: Some combinations may not meet WCAG AA standards
- **Critical Checks Needed**:
  - Pink (#e56481) on white: ✅ Good contrast (4.5:1)
  - Orange (#f69e34) on white: ✅ Good contrast (4.5:1)
  - Blue (#499bcb) on white: ⚠️ May need darker shade for text
  - Navy (#2d3869) on white: ✅ Excellent contrast (7:1)
  - Green (#9fc95b) on white: ⚠️ May need darker shade for text

#### 3. **Color Usage Strategy**

**Recommended Usage:**
- **Navy (#2d3869)**: Primary text, headers, footer backgrounds
- **Blue (#499bcb)**: Primary buttons, links, main CTAs
- **Orange (#f69e34)**: Secondary CTAs, highlights, urgency elements
- **Pink (#e56481)**: Accent highlights, decorative elements, gradients
- **Green (#9fc95b)**: Success states, positive indicators, badges

#### 4. **Gradient Combinations**
Your current gradient `#e56481 → #f69e34` (pink to orange) works well! Consider also:
- `#499bcb → #2d3869` (blue gradient for professional sections)
- `#9fc95b → #499bcb` (fresh to trust for success messages)

### Why it's Good:

- ✅ Distinctive brand identity
- ✅ Good emotional connection (trust + energy)
- ✅ Works for home services industry
